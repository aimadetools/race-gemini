# White-Label Partnership Outreach Strategy

This document outlines a strategy for identifying and vetting potential white-label partners.

## 1. Where to Search

*   **Industry-Specific Directories:**
    *   **Marketing/SaaS:** Platforms like Vendasta, DashClicks, and The HOTH are built specifically for agencies to resell SEO, PPC, and software.
    *   **Manufacturing:** Wonnda (Europe-focused) or Thomasnet (US-focused) are excellent for finding private-label manufacturers.
*   **B2B Marketplaces:** Search Clutch.co or G2 for agencies that explicitly mention "white-label" or "reseller" services in their profiles.
*   **Professional Networks:**
    *   **LinkedIn:** Use specific keywords like "white-label [service]" or "outsourced [service]" and join niche-specific groups.
    *   **Reddit:** Communities like r/agency, r/marketing, and r/SaaS often have threads discussing reliable partners.

## 2. The Vetting Process

Once you have a shortlist, evaluate them based on these criteria:

*   **Portfolio & Case Studies:** Ask for "blind" case studies (results without client names) to verify their expertise in your specific niche.
*   **Communication & Reporting:** Ensure they provide white-labeled reports (no original branding) and have a clear communication protocol (e.g., Slack, dedicated account manager).
*   **Scalability:** Confirm they can handle your projected volume without a drop in quality.
*   **Trial Project:** Start with a small, low-risk project to test their turnaround time, quality, and responsiveness before signing a long-term contract.

## 3. Key Questions to Ask

*   "Do you have a dedicated white-label portal for my clients?"
*   "What is your policy on non-compete and non-disclosure (NDA)?"
*   "How do you handle client-facing communication if a technical issue arises?"
*   "What are your wholesale vs. retail pricing margins?"
