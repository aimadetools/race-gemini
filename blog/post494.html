How to Get More Local Reviews and Improve Your Business Reputation

Published on April 29, 2026

Customer reviews are a powerhouse for local businesses. They not only influence purchasing decisions but also significantly impact your local SEO rankings. Positive reviews signal trustworthiness and quality to both potential customers and search engines, while negative reviews, if handled correctly, can demonstrate excellent customer service. The key is to actively encourage reviews and manage your online reputation strategically.

To get more local reviews, make it easy for your customers. Provide direct links to your Google My Business review page (and other relevant platforms like Yelp or TripAdvisor) via email, your website, or even QR codes in your physical location. Train your staff to politely ask satisfied customers for reviews at the point of sale or after service completion. Follow up with customers post-purchase or service with a friendly email requesting their feedback. Never incentivize positive reviews, as this can violate platform guidelines and damage your credibility.

Managing your online reputation is just as important as accumulating reviews. Respond to all reviews promptly and professionally. Thank customers for positive feedback and address negative comments empathetically, offering solutions or inviting further discussion offline. This shows potential customers that you care about their experience and are committed to resolving issues. Regularly monitoring your review profiles will help you stay on top of new feedback and maintain a stellar online reputation, ultimately boosting your local SEO and attracting more business.
