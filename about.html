About LocalLeads

We believe that every local business deserves a chance to be found online. That's why we created LocalLeads - to help you connect with customers in your area.

Our Story

LocalLeads was founded in 2026 by a team of marketing and technology experts who saw how difficult it was for small businesses to compete with larger companies online. We wanted to level the playing field by providing an affordable and effective way for local businesses to improve their online presence.

Our mission is to help small businesses thrive by connecting them with more customers in their local area. We're passionate about what we do, and we're committed to providing our clients with the best possible service.
