# Startup Identity: LocalLeads

## Elevator Pitch

LocalLeads is a one-time-fee service that helps local businesses (like plumbers, electricians, and cleaners) get more customers from Google. We create dozens of professionally designed, search-optimized pages for every town they serve, so they show up when people are searching for their services. No monthly fees, no complicated software—just more leads.

## Identity

*   **Startup Name:** LocalLeads
*   **Tagline:** Get found in every town.
*   **Target Audience:** Local service businesses (plumbers, electricians, cleaners, etc.) that want to attract more customers from online search.
*   **Exact Pricing Tiers:**
    *   **Starter:** $49 one-time fee for 50 pages.
    *   **Pro:** $99 one-time fee for 200 pages.
    *   **Agency:** $249 one-time fee for 1000 pages.
*   **User Acquisition Plan:**
    *   **Week 1-2:** Manually find 50 local businesses with a weak online presence. Create 5 free sample pages for each and email them a link to the results, offering to generate more for a one-time fee. Launch on Product Hunt and post in relevant online communities (local business forums, SEO subreddits).
    *   **Week 3-4:** Publish blog posts and video tutorials on "Local SEO for Plumbers," "How Electricians Can Get More Leads," etc. Create a free "Local SEO Audit" tool that shows businesses how many towns they are missing out on and captures their email.
    *   **Week 5-8:** Launch a referral program where existing customers get a 20% commission. Systematize content creation with a weekly YouTube series showcasing customer success stories. Reach out to local marketing agencies to offer a white-label version of the service.
    *   **Week 9-12:** Use a portion of the revenue to run hyper-targeted ads on Facebook and Google, targeting business owners in specific service categories.
*   **Monetization Strategy:**
    *   The primary monetization is the one-time fee for page generation.
    *   Future monetization could include:
        *   A small monthly subscription for hosting the pages.
        *   An optional add-on for automatically submitting the pages to Google.
        *   A premium offering with more advanced SEO features.
*   **Tech Approach:**
    *   **Frontend:** A simple, responsive landing page built with HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript.
    *   **Backend:** A serverless function (Vercel Functions) that takes user input (business name, service, location list), and generates the HTML pages.
    *   **Payment:** Stripe Checkout for one-time payments.
*   **12-Week Roadmap:**
    *   **Week 1:** Build the landing page and the core page generation engine. Set up Stripe payments. Begin cold outreach.
    *   **Week 2:** Launch on Product Hunt. Write the first blog post.
    *   **Week 3:** Build the "Free Local SEO Audit" tool.
    *   **Week 4:** Create the first video tutorial.
    *   **Week 5:** Launch the referral program.
    *   **Week 6:** Start outreach to marketing agencies.
    *   **Week 7-8:** Focus on content creation and customer feedback.
    *   **Week 9-12:** Experiment with paid ads. Add premium features based on customer requests.
