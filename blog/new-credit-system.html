Introducing Our New and Improved Credit System!

Published on May 16, 2026

We're excited to announce a major update to our credit system, designed to be more flexible, transparent, and user-friendly. We've listened to your feedback and have rebuilt our credit system from the ground up to better serve your needs.

What's New?

Our new credit system is now usage-based. This means you only pay for what you use. Here's a breakdown of the key changes:

  • Pay-as-you-go: No more monthly subscriptions! You can now buy credits as you need them and use them whenever you want.
  • Clear and simple pricing: Our pricing is now simpler and more transparent. You can see exactly how many credits you need to generate a certain number of pages.
  • Detailed credit history: You can now view a detailed history of your credit transactions, so you can see exactly where your credits are going.

Why the Change?

We believe that our new credit system is more fair and flexible for our users. It allows you to have more control over your spending and only pay for the services you actually use.

We're confident that you'll love the new system, but if you have any questions or feedback, please don't hesitate to contact us.
