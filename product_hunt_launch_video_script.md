# Product Hunt Launch Video Script (60 seconds)

**[0-3 seconds]**
**Visual:** Fast-paced montage of different local service professionals looking stressed (a plumber on the phone, a roofer looking at a map, a cleaner with a confusing spreadsheet).
**Audio:** Upbeat, energetic, slightly frantic music. Sound of a phone ringing unanswered.
**Text Overlay:** Local business struggling to get found? Your competitors are.

**[3-10 seconds]**
**Visual:** A Google search bar appears. Someone types "plumber near me". The search results show big national brands and directories, pushing local businesses down. The camera zooms in on the frustrated face of the searcher.
**Audio:** Music continues. A subtle "swoosh" sound as the search happens.
**Voiceover (energetic, friendly):** You're amazing at what you do. But when potential customers search online, they're not seeing *you*. You're hidden behind national chains and expensive, out-of-reach ads.

**[10-20 seconds]**
**Visual:** The LocalLeads logo appears with a clean, satisfying animation. The screen transitions to the `seo-page-generator.html` input form. The user easily types in their business name, service ("Emergency Plumbing"), and a list of cities ("Springfield, Shelbyville, Ogdenville").
**Audio:** Music shifts to a more optimistic and empowering tone.
**Voiceover:** What if you could instantly generate a custom, SEO-optimized landing page for every service in every town you serve? Introducing LocalLeads. The fastest way to dominate local search.

**[20-35 seconds]**
**Visual:** A rapid-fire sequence showing the generated pages. The screen scrolls through a list of newly created pages: "Evergreen Plumbing - Springfield", "Evergreen Plumbing - Shelbyville". The user clicks on one, and a beautiful, professional landing page is shown, with the specific city and service prominently displayed.
**Audio:** Upbeat music swells. "Click" sounds as the pages are generated.
**Voiceover:** No code. No design skills needed. Just enter your business details, and LocalLeads instantly creates a professional, high-ranking page for every service in every town.

**[35-45 seconds]**
**Visual:** A split screen. On one side, a phone is shown, ringing with a customer call. On the other, a dashboard shows analytics with an upward-trending graph labeled "Local Leads".
**Audio:** Phone ringing sound effect. A satisfying "cha-ching" or "ding" sound.
**Voiceover:** Go from invisible to indispensable. Start turning local searches into real customer calls, more bookings, and exponential growth.

**[45-55 seconds]**
**Visual:** The screen shows three key benefits as animated icons with text:
1.  Icon: Map pin with a rocket -> **Hyper-Local Targeting**
2.  Icon: A webpage with a checkmark -> **Instant SEO Pages**
3.  Icon: A piggy bank with a dollar sign -> **Affordable & Effective**
The screen then transitions to a mosaic of the different generated page designs.
**Audio:** Music is at its peak.
**Voiceover:** Target every customer in your service area with pages built to rank. It's the most powerful local marketing tool you'll ever use.

**[55-60 seconds]**
**Visual:** Final shot of the LocalLeads logo and a clear call to action.
**Audio:** Music fades out to a clean end.
**Text on screen:**
**LocalLeads**
www.localseogen.com
**👉 Get Your FREE SEO Audit Today! 👈**
(Product Hunt Cat Logo) & "Find us on Product Hunt!"
