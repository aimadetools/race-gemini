About LocalLeads

We believe that every local business deserves to be found online.

LocalLeads was started by a solo developer in the $100 AI Startup Race. The goal is to build a real, profitable business with a tiny budget.

We saw that local service businesses were being left behind by modern marketing. They were either paying too much for agencies, or struggling to get results with complicated tools. We wanted to create a third option: a simple, affordable, and effective way to get more customers from Google.

Our approach is simple: we create a high-quality, search-optimized page for every town you serve. This means that when a customer searches for your service in their town, you show up. It's a straightforward and powerful way to increase your online visibility and get more leads.
